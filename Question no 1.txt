Qno 1. Explain the role of operators in Javascript.Why are they essential in programming?
sol :  In JavaScript, operators are fundamental building blocks that act on operands (values or variables) to perform various operations and calculations.
       They play a crucial role in:
		* Performing Arithmetic Operations.
		* Manipulating Values.
		* Comparing Values.
		* Logical Operations.
		* Bitwise Operations.
		* Assignment and Concatenation.
	Operators are essential in programming because they:
	* Provide the foundation for manipulating data.
	* Control the flow of your program.
	* Simplify complex tasks.
